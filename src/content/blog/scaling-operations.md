Most agencies scale by adding people. We scaled by adding systems. Here's the operational framework that let us 3x revenue without 3x-ing the team.

## The Scaling Problem

Linear scaling — where revenue and headcount grow at the same rate — is a trap. Your margins stay flat, complexity compounds, and you end up managing people instead of building a business.

> "The goal isn't to build a bigger agency. It's to build a more leveraged one."

## The Three Pillars

### 1. Documented Processes

Every repeatable task gets a documented process. Not a 50-page SOP — a clear, concise checklist that any competent person can follow. We use Loom videos + Notion checklists.

### 2. Automated Handoffs

The space between steps is where things break. We automated every handoff: deal closed → project created → team notified → client onboarded. Zero manual steps, zero dropped balls.

### 3. Async Communication

Meetings are the enemy of scale. We default to async: Loom for updates, Slack for decisions, Linear for task management. Meetings happen only when alignment is genuinely needed.

## The Results

- Team size: 4 → 6 (50% increase)
- Revenue: $200k → $600k ARR (200% increase)
- Client satisfaction: maintained at 9.2/10 NPS
- Average workweek: reduced from 55 to 42 hours

The systems don't just scale the business — they scale your quality of life.
