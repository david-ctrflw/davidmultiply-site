Building a 7-Figure Agency in Public

David Multiply

Oct 24, 2023

Most agencies are built behind closed doors, hidden from the prying eyes of competitors. I decided to take a different path. In this post, I'm peeling back the curtain on how we scaled from zero to $1M ARR in just 14 months.

Phase 1: The Foundation of Focus

In the beginning, we tried to be everything to everyone. We offered SEO, PPC, Social Media, and Web Design. We were a "full-service" agency that was failing at everything.

"Specialization is not a limitation; it is a multiplier of your value proposition."

The turning point came when we cut 80% of our services. We focused exclusively on performance marketing for B2B SaaS. Within 3 months, our conversion rate on cold outreach doubled.

Phase 2: Productizing Service Delivery

Scaling requires systems. If every client project requires you to reinvent the wheel, you'll hit a ceiling quickly. We built a library of "Standard Operating Procedures" (SOPs) that allowed us to maintain quality while increasing volume.

  • check_circle Modular onboarding process reducing kick-off time by 40%.
  • check_circle Automated reporting dashboards via Looker Studio.
  • check_circle Weekly feedback loops between sales and delivery teams.
